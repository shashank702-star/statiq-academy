Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your data analytics career.

Do I need a math or coding background to enroll?

+
No. Our curriculum starts from absolute foundations. We begin with core probability concepts and basic SQL queries before introducing advanced programming. Approximately 60% of our students come from non-technical backgrounds.

How long does the program take to complete?

+
The program contains 300 hours of learning. If you dedicate 15 hours per week, you will graduate in 20 weeks. If you study full-time (30 hours per week), you can complete the entire program in 10 weeks.

Is this program really free?

+
Yes. StatIQ Data Academy is 100% free and open-source. There are no hidden subscription fees, paywalls, or upfront costs. All of our code tutorials, practice datasets, and structured syllabus modules are freely accessible to anyone wanting to learn data science.

Is there a certificate of completion?

+
Yes. Upon finishing all capstone projects, you will receive a verified digital credential that links directly to your project portfolios. This can be embedded directly in your LinkedIn profile or resume.
